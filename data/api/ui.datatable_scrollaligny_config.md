scrollAlignY
=============


@short: enables/disables scrolling the table just by whole rows (i.e. you won't be allowed to scroll along the full length of rows)

@type: boolean

@example:
webix.ui({
	view:"datatable",
	scrollAlignY:false,
	...
});

@template:	api_config
@defined:	ui.datatable
