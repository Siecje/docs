##Overview

UI-related **Sidemenu** inherits from [list](desktop/list.md) and lets users select the necessary item from the list of grouped items and see some content in on the page. 
Each element of a submenu can start its own submenu. <br>
A dynamic **Submenu** becomes visible only when you put a mouse pointer over the corresponding item of the main menu (on 'onMouseOver' event) and hides as soon as you remove mouse pointer ("onMouseOut").
