##Overview

UI-related <a href="http://webix.com/widget/calendar/" title="webix calendar widget">calendar</a> 
inherits from [view](desktop/view.md) and allows you to include the Date and Time picker calendar into an app.

It is a handy and fully interactive tool to pick the necessary time and date when filling a form. 
The dedicated timepicker allows setting the necessary time that will be displayed below the calendar area.
